How it works

Here is an overview of how we approach each new project.

Drone

  • Launch drone
  • Drive slowly around track as drone follows and gathers data
  • Drone land when 1 lap is complete

Race

  • Race normally as the car assembles data

Review

  • After each lap, review your performance
  • Compare your performance between laps at different segments
  • Create your best lap
  • Share with friends

Map your race

Track your stats and personal records. Go through your lap history across different segments of the race track. Keep track of the stats at your best segments.

Detailed position tracking

Know your exact position on the track from each lap.

Using a drone, we collect GPS data of the entire track, including road edge detection, bank angle, and elevation change. Can't find a track? Feel free to create your own course by having a drone follow your car.

Improve your lap time

Overlay your composite best lap on the track and compare against the calculated optimal driving line.

Grabbnig your best perfomrance along each segment of a racetrack, we stitch together your perfect lap. Compare the line of your perfect lap against our calculated best line.

Race against your friends and pros

Compare your performance against friends and racetrack leaderboards.

Share your race day with your friend, comparing best lap and segment times. See how you stack up against the pros on the leaderbaords.
